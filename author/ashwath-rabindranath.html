Playing with Consumer Data

In this entry, we'll play with the data from the Consumer Complaint Database of the CFPB (Consumer Financial Protection Bureau). This publicly available database tracks complaints made to the agency regarding financial products, from the date of the complaint and the issue at stake, to the company's response (timely or otherwise) and finally to the consumer's reaction to the response -- whether or not they chose to dispute it. The data can be found at https://www.consumerfinance.gov/data-research/consumer-complaints/#download-the-data
